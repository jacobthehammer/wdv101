The Founders

The Last Coffee House was founded in 2006 by MSU graduate students Michel Bohmer and Sandy Thomas. Having spent countless hours studying in coffee shops, they knew exactly what the perfect campus spot needed: great coffee, good food, and a vibe that fosters both creativity and relaxation.

What We Serve

We take pride in serving premium products. It starts with our fresh roasted coffee sourced from the best coffee sources around the world. But we don't stop at coffee.

Our menu includes:

  • Hot and cold teas
  • Grilled and cold sandwiches
  • Homemade soups
  • A wide variety of cakes, pastries, and desserts

Visiting us in the evening? We offer a selection of wine and beer, transforming into a relaxed lounge where you can unwind after a long day of classes.
