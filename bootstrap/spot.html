Spot

The true commander of Lt. Commander Data's quarters.

Biography

Spot was a domesticated cat who was Data's pet in the 2360s and 2370s. She lived with him while he served aboard the USS Enterprise-D and -E. She had a litter of kittens with one of the other cats on the Enterprise-D. Data once wrote a poem about her, entitled "Ode to Spot". (TNG: "Schisms", "Genesis")

Behind the Scenes: It is important to note that Spot was portrayed by multiple cats over the course of The Next Generation and Star Trek: Picard. The cats varied in breed and gender (sometimes a Somali cat, sometimes an orange tabby), creating some continuity errors regarding Spot's gender and appearance!
